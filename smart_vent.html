Smart Air Vent

The Story

Despite central air-conditioning units constantly improving, one of their biggest downfalls is the problem of air flow. Regardless of how powerful of an air conditioning unit is, the upper floor is always hotter in the summer, and cooler in the winter. This is due to the airflow that weakens while coming to the top floor. Hence, when it comes time to cool or heat the room, the air barely reaches and it becomes ineffective. This leads to forcing the heating/cooling system to run for longer periods of time; wearing the machines down quicker while costing you a higher energy bill. A fix to this might be to purchase solo air conditioners, but those can cost thousands of dollars!

This is where the smart vent comes in. The smart vent uses a built in fan powered by batteries that helps to boost the idle air towards the room which allows for real temperature control! That’s not all, the smart vent also has automated vent control! This means that if the air conditioning is not on, the vents will close up so that debris can not fall into the vents, avoiding the cost of duct cleaning. The smart vent also has the ability to be controlled by the user from wherever they are, and the temperature can be monitored too!

Features

The smart vent is full of features that helps the user maintain the temperature they wanted from the start throughout their home. They come together to provide a great user experience and help the user save lots of money compared to purchasing solo air conditioners or upgrading their current central air conditioning system.

Smart Air Circulation Booster

A 9mm fan boosts the air flow from the air conditioning system up to 60% more to your room.

Smart Flaps

Opens up when the air conditioning is on, and closes up when its not needed through your devices or automation.

Smartphone and Computer Controls

You can use your smartphone and computer to control which rooms get the cool air by using the smart flaps and monitor the current temperature using the built in temperature sensor.

Easy Maintenance

Whenever it comes time to cleaning your vent, it will be easy as 123 with just a cover to open.

Debris Protection

The smart flaps of the vent close up when it gets hot just so you can spend less money towards duct cleaning. This is because the sealed vent lets you just take the vent and dump any debris that has fallen onto it.

No Cables

The smart vent automatically calibrates the temperature to ensure you get the best possible results. It will be powered by batteries, allowing for a true wireless experience.

More About the App Interface

In order to have the best compatibility, we used Cayenne as the home where you can connect your devices to the vent. It allows you to view the temperature, open and close the vents, and lets you turn the air circulation booster on/off.
